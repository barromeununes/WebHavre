colors: purple, red, white, green, blue

turn overview:
1: supply: place your ship piece on the next supply space. place 2 different goods/francs shown on the offer spaces
2: main action, choose either:
   a: take all goods/francs from one offer space
   b: move your person to a new building (e.g. to build new buildings).

additional actions:
buy: buy buildings and ships at any time during your turn
sell: sell buildings and ships for half face value at any time during the game
repay loans: return loan cards for 5 francs each at any time during the game

loans:
a loan is -7 points, gives you 4 francs, and must be repaid with 5 francs.
you can only take a loan when something requires you to pay interest, or to pay for food during a harvest.
